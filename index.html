Beddy Butler

Beddy butler is a small applet utility for OS X. It is designed to help nudge you to go to bed rather than stay awake on your computer.

Usage

Setup: You can change the following settings:

  • Set a time frame for your desired time to prepare to go to bed (e.g. 10:30 - 11:00)
  • Choose your applet behaviour from a set of butlers: Shy, Insistent and Zombie.
  • You are also be able to preview each butler sound
  • Configure whether you would like the applet to launch at login (this setting will be ON by default)
  • Tell it 'Not Now Old Chap' to silence the app for that evening.
